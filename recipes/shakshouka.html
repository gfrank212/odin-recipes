Shakshouka

Description

Can't think of a better hangover cure than scooping spicy shakshouka straight out of the cast iron pan with a crusty bread. This one is a crowd pleaser.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • 1 onion - chopped
  • 1 bell pepper - thinly sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic - thinly sliced
  • 1 to 2 jalapeno peppers - diced
  • 2 cans skinned tomatoes - crushed/blended
  • ground cumin
  • paprika
  • salt and pepper
  • 5 large eggs

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over high heat. Cook peppers and onion, allow them to char. Reduce heat to medium and add in garlic, cook until fragrant.
  2. Add crushed tomatoes and seasoning to the pan. Simmer, uncovered, for approximately 10 minutes.
  3. Make indentations in the tomato mixture for each egg. Crack egs into indentations and cover the edges with some of the tomato mixture. Cover the skillet with a lid and cook until eggs set, careful not to overcook.
  4. Serve directly from the pan with a crusty bread.
