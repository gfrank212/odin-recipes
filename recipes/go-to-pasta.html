Go-To Pasta

Description

This is my go-to pasta whenever I need something easy and delicious. The ingredients are on my shopping list every single time I go to the grocery store so I always have what I need on hand at a moments notice. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • 0.5 to 1 lbs of chicken breast
  • 2 cups of cherry tomatoes - halved
  • 1 shallot - diced
  • 1 clove of garlic - diced
  • Juice from 1 lemon
  • 1/2 cup of heavy cream
  • 2 tbs of butter
  • 2 tbs olive oil
  • parsley - roughly chopped
  • parmesan cheese - grated
  • chili flakes, salt, pepper

Steps

  1. Prep all ingredients before starting to cook
  2. Bring a pot of water to a boil. Cook pasta.
  3. Season chicken with salt and pepper, cayenne pepper for spice if desired. Heat a drizzle of olive oil over medium heat. Cook chicken until done, set aside to rest.
  4. In the same pan, heat the butter and cook the garlic and shallots until fragrant. Approx 1 minute. Add the halved tomatoes and some chili flakes. Cook for an additional minute or until tomatoes begin soften, don't overcook.
  5. Squeeze lemon juice into the pan and add the heavy cream. Stir until combined and reduce heat. Salt and pepper to taste
  6. Slice chicken and mix back into the sauce. Bring to a simmer, then add cooked pasta. Turn off the heat and stir to combine. Top with chopped parsley and parmesan cheese.
