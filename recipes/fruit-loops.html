Froot Loops

Description

Breakfast? Dinner? Snack time? A big bowl of froot loops and milk is always an appropriate choice.

Ingredients

  • Froot Loops cereal
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Add cereal to very large bowl.
  2. Pour milk into bowl just until the cereal begins to float.
  3. Enjoy. Add extra cereal or milk to desired ratio.
